Consistency contract and boundary behavior

The consistency contract defines what the system may emit at the edge of its computational budget. It specifies when conclusions are robust, conditional, or indeterminate.

Closure runs in two phases, propagation and verification. Strict mode prunes inconsistent branches, while exploratory mode keeps them for analysis without letting them influence final answers.

When resources are exhausted, the system must degrade gracefully. It does not hallucinate; it either returns a conditional statement or reports that the budget was insufficient.

VSAVM is an Axiologic Research experiment within the Achilles project. This static documentation is written in clear academic English for engineers.
