Faithful surface realization

Decoding is a surface realization of internal state rather than a new source of knowledge. The VM produces a verdict or structured object, and the realizer renders it into text or modality-compatible events.

This separation is essential for trust. The realizer is allowed to choose phrasing and format, but it is not allowed to invent facts that were never established in the VM.

For multimodal output, the same rule holds. The system emits structured event streams, relations, or plans that correspond directly to execution traces.

VSAVM is an Axiologic Research experiment within the Achilles project. This static documentation is written in clear academic English for engineers.
