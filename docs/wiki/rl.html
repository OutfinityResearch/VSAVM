Reinforcement Learning (RL)

RL is used as a shaping mechanism rather than a primary training objective. The system receives feedback for choosing program candidates and response modes that remain consistent under bounded closure.

This keeps the action space small and interpretable. It avoids costly token-level RL while still guiding the system toward robust interpretations.

Negative signals arise when conclusions conflict under closure, nudging future decisions away from unstable programs.
