Consistency contract

The consistency contract defines when the system is allowed to emit a conclusion. It binds answers to the outcome of bounded closure rather than to surface confidence.

Budgets are explicit and configurable. This makes the system monotonic with respect to search effort and allows deeper analysis on demand.

The contract enforces honest degradation to conditional or indeterminate results when resources are insufficient.
