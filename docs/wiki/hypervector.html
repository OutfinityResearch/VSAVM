Hypervector

A hypervector is a high-dimensional representation used by VSA. It is generated deterministically, and combined through operations such as binding and bundling for retrieval.

External link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyperdimensional_computing.

VSAVM is an Axiologic Research experiment within the Achilles project. This static documentation is written in clear academic English for engineers.
