Canonicalization

Canonicalization maps surface statements into a normalized internal representation. It is the basis for reliable contradiction detection across paraphrases.

In VSAVM, canonical forms include typed slots and explicit negation, allowing the VM to check conflicts deterministically.

Without canonicalization, bounded closure would be ineffective because equivalent claims would appear unrelated.
