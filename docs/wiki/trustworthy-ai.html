Trustworthy AI

Trustworthy AI in VSAVM relies on constrained emission and visible execution. Answers are tied to VM state and bounded closure rather than to probabilistic fluency alone.

The execution log provides an audit trail that explains what was explored and what conflicts were detected. This makes trust a measurable property.

When a result is conditional, the system states its assumptions explicitly, which preserves user control and clarity.

External link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Explainable_artificial_intelligence.

VSAVM is an Axiologic Research experiment within the Achilles project. This static documentation is written in clear academic English for engineers.
