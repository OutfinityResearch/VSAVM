Symbolic execution

Symbolic execution runs programs over symbols and constraints rather than concrete values. This makes it suitable for exploring multiple branches of reasoning.

The VSAVM VM uses symbolic execution to test alternative interpretations and to detect inconsistencies early in the search.

The approach supports bounded closure by making the branching factor explicit and manageable.
